ABOUT US

RUE’s Lens was founded by three college students taking up their bachelor's in 2017, these fine young people, although studying different degrees, had a common love and passion for photography, which is what brought this service and team together. At first, RUE’s Lens was made as a way to earn a small income to support them in their future endeavors, but in late 2019, as the news of the COVID-19 virus surged, the three college students decided to expand their family of three and welcomed new members from all over the world. This allowed for our services to be opened for almost every country out there, and opened new opportunities for young and old talents alike. Though working remotely from each other we as a team strive to be connected and work in harmony and according to our mission - to make memories that last.
